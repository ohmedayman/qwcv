What is QCV?

QCV is a premium AI-powered CV/resume builder competing with tadween.me. It offers 15+ professional ATS-friendly templates, AI scoring, job matching, public portfolio pages, and a full admin panel. Hosted on GitHub Pages with Firebase backend and custom domain via Cloudflare.

Key Features

14 AI Features

Summary, proofread, improve, skills, ATS optimize, translate, cover letter, tailor, skill gap, interview prep, LinkedIn import, template recommend, CV analysis, career advisor

15 CV Templates

Classic, Modern, Minimal, ATS-Safe, Creative, Executive, Elegant, Bold, Professional, Timeline, Infographic, Photo, Two-Column, Monochrome, and more

PDF Export

High-quality PDF with html2pdf.js. Free: 1 download with watermark. Pro: 5 clean exports. Unlimited: unlimited

LinkedIn Import

Paste LinkedIn URL, AI extracts and auto-fills name, title, experience, skills, education

CV Score & ATS

Real-time scoring: Contact, Content, ATS, Skills, Impact. Detailed recommendations

Portfolio & QR

Public portfolio links, My Page, QR code for easy sharing, PDF download for employers

Version History

Auto-saves versions on every save. Restore any previous version with one click

Job Tracker

Track applications: company, position, status (Applied/Interview/Offer/Rejected), notes

CV Comparison

Compare two CV versions side by side with AI deep analysis and improvement scoring
